1. Pumpkin Seeds Salads

Ingredients:

To make a delicious pumpkin seed salad, start with a base of mixed greens such as spinach, arugula, or lettuce. Add in some roasted or raw pumpkin seeds for a crunchy texture, and toss in halved cherry tomatoes, cucumber slices, and thinly sliced red onion for freshness. Dice up some avocado for creaminess, and crumble in feta or goat cheese if desired. For extra flavor, include sliced olives, shredded or julienned carrots, and diced bell peppers. You can also add fresh herbs like parsley, cilantro, or basil to enhance the taste. For the dressing, mix olive oil with balsamic vinegar or lemon juice, a touch of honey or maple syrup for sweetness, and season with salt, pepper, and optional mustard or garlic powder for extra flavor. This versatile salad can be adjusted to suit your preferences or what you have on hand.


2. Oyesters

Ingredients:

For a delicious oyster dish, you’ll need fresh oysters (either shucked or in the shell, depending on your preference). Start by melting some unsalted butter in a pan and adding minced garlic, allowing it to infuse the butter with flavor. Squeeze in fresh lemon juice and zest for a tangy kick, then stir in chopped parsley for a burst of freshness. If you like a bit of texture, add breadcrumbs (either panko or regular) and grated Parmesan cheese for a rich, crispy topping. For added depth of flavor, a splash of white wine can be included, along with a few dashes of hot sauce for heat. You can also incorporate finely diced shallots for a mild onion flavor and garnish with chopped scallions. Season with black pepper, salt, and optional cayenne pepper or paprika for extra spice. This combination of ingredients is perfect for preparing baked, grilled, or sautéed oysters, offering a flavorful balance of richness, tang, and texture.


3. Shrimps

Ingredients:

To prepare a flavorful shrimp dish, you’ll need fresh or frozen shrimp, peeled and deveined. Start by seasoning the shrimp with salt, black pepper, and a pinch of cayenne pepper or paprika for some spice. Heat olive oil or butter in a pan and sauté minced garlic until fragrant. You can also add finely chopped shallots or onions for extra flavor. For a zesty kick, squeeze in some fresh lemon juice, and optionally add lemon zest. To enhance the dish, a splash of white wine or chicken broth can be added, allowing the flavors to meld together. Fresh herbs like parsley or basil can be stirred in for freshness, while a pinch of red pepper flakes can be included for an added layer of heat. If you prefer a creamy shrimp dish, a splash of heavy cream or coconut milk can be mixed in. Serve the shrimp over pasta, rice, or a bed of greens for a complete meal.


4. Berry Spinach Smoothie

Ingredients:

To make a refreshing Berry Spinach Smoothie, you’ll need a handful of fresh spinach leaves as the base, which provides a nutrient-rich green boost. Add a variety of mixed berries such as strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries for sweetness and antioxidants. For creaminess, include a banana, which also adds natural sweetness. To make the smoothie more refreshing and hydrating, you can incorporate coconut water or almond milk. For an extra boost of nutrition, add a tablespoon of chia seeds, flaxseeds, or protein powder if desired. A drizzle of honey or maple syrup can be added for sweetness, though the fruits will typically provide enough. Finally, blend everything with a few ice cubes or frozen fruit to make the smoothie cold and thick. This combination of ingredients creates a delicious, nutrient-packed drink perfect for breakfast or a post-workout snack.


5. Grilled Salmon with Roasted Vegetables

Ingredients:

For a delicious Grilled Salmon with Roasted Vegetables, start with fresh salmon fillets, seasoned with olive oil, salt, black pepper, and a squeeze of lemon juice. You can add some fresh herbs like dill, parsley, or thyme for extra flavor. For the roasted vegetables, choose a variety of colorful options such as carrots, bell peppers, zucchini, and red onion. Toss them in olive oil, salt, pepper, and your favorite herbs or spices, like rosemary or garlic powder. Roast the vegetables in the oven until tender and caramelized. You can also add a drizzle of balsamic vinegar or a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese over the vegetables for an extra layer of flavor. Once the salmon is grilled to perfection, serve it alongside the roasted veggies for a healthy and satisfying meal.


6. Green Teà

Ingredients:

To prepare a simple and refreshing cup of green tea, you’ll need green tea leaves or a green tea bag. Start by heating water to just below boiling, around 170-180°F (77-82°C), as water that’s too hot can make the tea taste bitter. Pour the hot water over the tea leaves or tea bag in a cup or teapot. Let it steep for about 2-3 minutes, allowing the leaves to release their delicate flavor. If desired, you can add a small amount of honey or lemon to enhance the taste. For extra freshness, a few fresh mint leaves can also be added. Stir gently and enjoy the soothing and healthy benefits of this aromatic beverage.


7. Mixed Berryies & Greek Yougurt

Ingredients:

To make a delicious Mixed Berries & Greek Yogurt dish, start with a combination of fresh or frozen mixed berries, such as strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, and blackberries, for a burst of sweetness and antioxidants. Add a generous scoop of Greek yogurt, which provides a creamy texture and a good dose of protein. For extra sweetness, drizzle honey or maple syrup over the top, though the natural sweetness of the berries may be enough. You can also sprinkle some granola or nuts for added crunch and texture. If you like, add a few chia seeds or flaxseeds for a nutritional boost. This simple yet flavorful combination makes for a healthy breakfast or a satisfying snack.


8. Watermelon Salad with chia and mint

Ingredients:

To prepare a refreshing Watermelon Salad with Chia and Mint, start with ripe, juicy watermelon, cut into cubes or chunks. Add a handful of fresh mint leaves, finely chopped, to enhance the flavor with a burst of freshness. For a nutritional boost, sprinkle in some chia seeds, which will add texture and provide healthy omega-3 fatty acids. You can also squeeze a bit of lime juice over the salad for a zesty kick, and a drizzle of honey or agave syrup can be added for sweetness, if desired. Toss everything together gently and serve chilled for a light and refreshing summer salad.
